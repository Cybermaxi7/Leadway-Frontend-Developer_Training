Chips, Egg, and Peppered Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chips

- 4 large potatoes (or use pre-cut frozen chips)
- Salt to taste
- Oil for frying

For the Egg

- 2 large eggs
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon butter or oil

For the Peppered Chicken

- 4 chicken drumsticks or thighs
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 bell peppers (any color), chopped
- 1-2 chili peppers, chopped (adjust to taste)
- 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Preparing the Chips

1. Peel and Cut Potatoes: Peel the potatoes and cut them into chips (fries) or use pre-cut frozen chips.
2. Rinse and Dry: Rinse the cut potatoes in cold water and pat them dry with a paper towel.
3. Heat Oil: In a deep fryer or large pot, heat the oil to 350°F (175°C).
4. Fry the Chips: Fry the chips in batches until golden and crispy, about 4-5 minutes per batch.
5. Drain and Season: Remove the chips from the oil and drain on paper towels. Season with salt while hot.

Preparing the Egg

1. Beat the Eggs: In a small bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
2. Heat the Pan: In a non-stick pan, melt the butter or heat the oil over medium heat.
3. Cook the Eggs: Pour the beaten eggs into the pan. Stir gently until the eggs are fully cooked but still soft, about 2-3 minutes.

Preparing the Peppered Chicken

1. Season the Chicken: Season the chicken pieces with salt, pepper, and paprika.
2. Brown the Chicken: In a large skillet or pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and brown them on all sides. Remove the chicken and set aside.
3. Cook the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the chopped onions and garlic. Sauté until the onions are translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
4. Add Peppers and Tomatoes: Add the bell peppers and chili peppers. Cook for another 2-3 minutes. Then add the crushed tomatoes and dried thyme.
5. Simmer: Return the chicken to the skillet. Cover and simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
6. Adjust Seasoning: Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.

Serving

1. Plate the Chips: Place a serving of chips on each plate.
2. Add the Eggs: Add a portion of scrambled eggs next to the chips.
3. Serve the Chicken: Place a piece of peppered chicken on each plate, along with some of the pepper sauce from the skillet.

Enjoy your delicious meal of chips, egg, and peppered chicken!
